Bounty is a sats incentive to encourage a good answer

  1. Bounty sender is anonymous
  3. Anyone can increase the current bounty
  4. Only signed answers can receive a bounty award
  5. Bounty is awarded to the signed answer with the most votes
  6. Bounty is awarded 3 days after the first signed answer is posted unless there is a better answer
  7. Every time a better answer is posted the 3 day timer starts over
  8. Bounty only applies to answers posted after the initial bounty was started. If bounty is increased, the time of increase is not taken into the account.
  9. If the answer author does not take custody of the bounty within 7 days the award becomes available to the next best answer
